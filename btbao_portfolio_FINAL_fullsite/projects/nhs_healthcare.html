Overview

Multi-year analysis of appointment volumes and utilisation, service-setting mix, mode split, DNA cost sizing, and weekday load balancing to surface operational opportunities.

Key Insights

  • Seasonal demand with utilisation peaks < ~88%; April troughs after holidays.
  • General Practice 70%+ of activity; extended access underutilised.
  • ~2/3 face-to-face, ~1/3 telephone; video negligible.
  • 8% DNA equates to ~742k lost clinician hours annually.
  • Rebalancing Mondays→Fridays and telehealth expansion reduce pressure.
