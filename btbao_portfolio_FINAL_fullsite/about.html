About

I’m Bobby Bao - a commercially minded account manager with a passion for data.

Working in media, advertising, and tech, I’ve seen how data sits at the heart of growth. From performance deep-dives that uncover upsell opportunities to running partner enablement sessions powered by insights, analytics consistently proved to be the lever that turns relationships into revenue.

Now, through my Data Analytics programme at the London School of Economics, I’ve built fluency in Python, SQL, R, BI tools, and machine learning - skills that allow me to unlock patterns, segment customers, and forecast outcomes.

My focus is on blending commercial acumen with technical rigour - helping businesses grow by making smarter, data-driven decisions.
